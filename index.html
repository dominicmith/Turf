Indoor nets are specialized netting systems used in indoor sports facilities.
They contain or separate different areas within the facility, ensuring safety and organization.

Constructed with durable materials like nylon or polyester, they are lightweight and easily adjustable.
Indoor nets are commonly used in sports like soccer, volleyball, tennis, badminton, and cricket.
